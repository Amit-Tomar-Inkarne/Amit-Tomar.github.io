About

Amit Tomar is a masters student at IIIT Bangalore , India. He is member of Graphic-Visualization-Computing Lab , working under the guidance of Prof. Jaya Sreevalsan-Nair . His current work involves modelling and visualizations for small world networks.

Before joining masters, he worked at Embedded Innovation Labs, Tata Consultancy Services for around two and half years. His work included design and development of UI and middleware frameworks for high end In-Vehicle Infotainment systems.
